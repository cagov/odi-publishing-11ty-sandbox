Pesticide Violations

Pesticide Violation allegations are handled by the local county Agricultural Commissioner’s Office.  To find your local ag commissioner please visit the CDFA Website. For more information on pesticide related claims please visit the California Department of Pesticide Regulation.

Odor, Light or Noise Complaints

Odor nuisance, light pollution or noise complaints are generally handled by your local city or county (depending on the local jurisdiction) Code Enforcement Department.  Find your county’s website information here:  https://www.counties.org/county-websites-profile-information. Or find your city’s website information here: https://www.counties.org/cities-within-each-county

Wildlife Complaints

Negative impact of on wildlife allegations are handled by the California Department of Fish and Wildlife. Members of the public remain our best source of information related to illegal cannabis cultivation. If you witness a polluting or poaching incident, a fish and game code violation, or have information about a violation, call CalTIP 1-888-334-CALTIP (888-334-2258) or https://wildlife.ca.gov/Enforcement/CalTIP 

May 15, 2023
Illegal Timber Harvest Complaints

Illegal Timber Harvest complaints are handled by the California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection. The California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection (CAL FIRE) enforces the laws that regulate logging on privately-owned lands in California.  You will need to contact a regional office from their website listing.
