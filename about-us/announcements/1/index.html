Pesticide Violations

Pesticide Violation allegations are handled by the local county Agricultural Commissioner’s Office.  To find your local ag commissioner please visit the CDFA Website. For more information on pesticide related claims please visit the California Department of Pesticide Regulation.

Odor, Light or Noise Complaints

Odor nuisance, light pollution or noise complaints are generally handled by your local city or county (depending on the local jurisdiction) Code Enforcement Department.  Find your county’s website information here:  https://www.counties.org/county-websites-profile-information. Or find your city’s website information here: https://www.counties.org/cities-within-each-county
