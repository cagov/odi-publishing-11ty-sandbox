Illegal Timber Harvest Complaints

Illegal Timber Harvest complaints are handled by the California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection. The California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection (CAL FIRE) enforces the laws that regulate logging on privately-owned lands in California.  You will need to contact a regional office, which is listed on the CAL FIRE website.
